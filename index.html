About Us
Who We Are

A passionate community of student engineers and innovators pushing the boundaries of robotics and technology.

Building the Future
One Robot at a Time

RoboSys is the official Robotics Club operations and audit platform. Founded by a group of dedicated students, our club brings together engineers, programmers, and creative thinkers to design, build, and compete with cutting-edge robotic systems.

We believe in radical transparency — every peso of club funds is tracked, verified, and publicly accessible. Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation, accountability, and continuous learning through hands-on robotics projects and competitions.
